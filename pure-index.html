What is a Block Party

Block Parties can range from simple gatherings in a neighbor's yard to neighborhood events including food and entertainment that occupy the entire street. They are allowed for only those residents of the block attending.

Block Parties allow you the opportunity to get to know your neighbors. Block parties are allowed up until 10:00 pm only.

Getting Started

First priority is for one neighbor to act as chairperson and complete the necessary paperwork which includes gathering neighbor signatures and also contacting any non-residential establishments in the immediate vicinity that would be affected by the closing of the street. The petition for a permit must be returned a minimum of 3 weeks prior to the block party.
